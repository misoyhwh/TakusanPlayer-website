Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

üöÄ Basic Usage

What is Takusan Player?

+

Takusan Player is a multi-video player application designed exclusively for Apple Vision Pro. It can simultaneously play up to 10 videos in 3D space, with each video managed in independent windows.

What video formats are supported?

+

Supports all video formats supported by AVKit, including MP4, MOV, M4V, QuickTime, and MPEG-4.

Where can I select videos from?

+

You can select videos from Photo Library, Documents folder, and other locations accessible through the Files app.

How many videos can be played simultaneously?

+

You can launch up to 10 video players simultaneously, each controlled independently.

üéÆ Features

What are the batch control features?

+

The "Play All", "Pause All", and "Seek All to Start" functions allow you to control all players at once, enabling efficient video management.

Can I enable loop playback?

+

Yes, you can enable/disable loop playback individually for each player. The loop status is visually indicated with a blue icon.

Tell me about the browser feature

+

The built-in WebKit browser allows access to video sites like YouTube. It includes back/forward/reload/home functionality.
