Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Takusan Player (the "App") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information Collection

1.1 No Personal Information Collection

Takusan Player does NOT collect the following personal information:

  • Personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Payment information
  • Any other personally identifiable information

1.2 Data Access

The App accesses the following data to provide functionality:

  • Video Files: Video files selected by you for playback
  • Photo Library: Access for video selection (selected files only)
  • Documents Folder: Access for video file selection

This data is processed only on your device and is never transmitted externally.

2. Data Usage

2.1 Purpose of Use

Accessed data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing video file playback functionality
  • Providing basic app features (playback controls, volume adjustment, etc.)
  • App operations in response to your actions

2.2 Data Storage

The App may store the following data locally:

  • App settings (volume settings, loop settings, etc.)
  • Recently used file references

This information is stored only on your Vision Pro device and is not transmitted externally.

3. Data Sharing

3.1 Third-Party Sharing

Takusan Player does not share your data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When disclosure is required by legal obligation
  • When you have given explicit consent
  • When necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of you or others

3.2 No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not perform the following:

  • Usage tracking or analytics
  • Advertisement display or tracking
  • Use of third-party analytics services
  • Automatic transmission of crash reports or telemetry data

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Use of Apple's security frameworks
  • Data processing within app sandbox
  • Implementation of secure file access features
  • Security design to prevent unauthorized access

4.2 Data Retention Period

The App retains data for the minimum necessary period:

  • Video files: Access only during app usage
  • Settings: Until app uninstallation
  • Temporary cache data: Deleted upon app termination

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will promptly delete such information.

If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

6. International Use

While the App is available worldwide, all data processing occurs on your device. No data is transferred across borders.

7. Your Rights

7.1 Data Management

You have the following rights:

  • Modify or delete app settings
  • Change file access permissions
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us with questions or requests

7.2 Permission Management

You can change the following permissions anytime through iOS/visionOS settings:

  • Photo Library access
  • Files and folders access

8. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Important changes will be communicated through:

  • Announcements on this website
  • In-app notifications (when applicable)
  • App Store update information

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

9. Third-Party Services

9.1 Built-in Browser

When using the App's built-in browser to access websites:

  • The privacy policies of accessed websites apply
  • The App does not record or track browser activities
  • Cookies set by websites are deleted when the browser session ends

9.2 Apple Services

The App uses the following Apple services:

  • App Store (for download and updates)
  • iOS/visionOS system services

For these services, Apple's Privacy Policy applies.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: misoyhwh@me.com

11. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 15, 2025.
